# Job Description: Mobile App Developer

## Position Details
- **Position:** Mobile App Developer
- **Department:** Technology / Engineering
- **Job Location:** Ahmedabad
- **Job Type:** Full-time (Work From Office)
- **Employment Type:** Full Time
- **Experience Level:** Mid Level (3+ years)
- **Number of Vacancies:** 1

---

## Job Overview

We are seeking an experienced Mobile App Developer to join our dynamic team in Ahmedabad. The ideal candidate will have strong expertise in mobile application development with at least 3 years of hands-on experience. This role requires proficiency in either Flutter (Android/iOS) or React Native, along with a proven track record of delivering high-quality mobile applications across various domains.

---

## Required Qualifications

### Educational Requirements
- Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or related field

### Experience Requirements
- Minimum 3+ years of experience working as a full-time Developer
- Must have executed at least 7-8 projects in different domains
- Strong technical knowledge about the mobile application & web world
- Proven experience in building and deploying production-ready mobile applications

---

## Role-Specific Requirements

### Option 1: Flutter + Android Developer

**Technical Skills:**
- Must have hands-on experience with Flutter & Android development
- In-depth knowledge of Core Java, Kotlin & Dart languages
- Strong knowledge of Flutter widgets for both iOS and Android platforms
- Expertise in Jetpack Compose for modern Android UI development
- Experience in building mobile applications utilizing web services (SOAP/REST/JSON/GSON)
- Knowledge of Play Store publishing & distribution processes
- Proficiency with third-party libraries and APIs integration
- Good knowledge of databases (MySQL, SQLite, Realm)

### Option 2: Flutter + iOS Developer

**Technical Skills:**
- Must have hands-on experience with Flutter & iOS development
- In-depth knowledge of Swift & Dart languages
- Strong knowledge of Flutter widgets for iOS and Android platforms
- Deep understanding of iOS SDK and Swift programming
- Proven experience developing iOS applications using SwiftUI and UIKit
- Experience in building mobile applications utilizing web services (SOAP/REST/JSON/GSON)
- Knowledge of App Store publishing & distribution processes
- Proficiency with third-party libraries and APIs integration
- Good knowledge of databases (MySQL, SQLite, Realm)

### Option 3: React Native Developer

**Technical Skills:**
- Must have hands-on experience with React Native framework
- Strong knowledge of Laravel framework
- In-depth knowledge of Core PHP
- Develop and maintain high-quality, scalable web and mobile applications using React.js and React Native
- Proficiency with Redux for state management
- Experience implementing React Hooks for better component functionality
- Experience working with Git, JIRA, and Agile Methodologies
- Knowledge of Test-Driven Development (TDD)
- Experience in building mobile applications utilizing web services (REST/JSON)
